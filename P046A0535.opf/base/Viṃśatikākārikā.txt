|| namaḥ sarvabuddhabodhisattvebhyaḥ ||viṃśatikākārikāvijñaptimātramevaitadasadarthāvabhāsanāt|yathā
taimirikasyāsatkeśacandrādidarśanam

yadi vijñaptiranarthā niyamo deśakālayoḥ |santānasyāniyamaśca yuktā kṛtyakriyā na ca

deśādiuniyamaḥ siddhaḥ svapnavat pretavat punaḥ|santānāniyamaḥ sarvaiḥ pūyanadyādidarśane

svapnopaghātavat kṛtyakriyā narakavat punaḥ|sarvaṃ narakapālādidarśane taiśca bādhane

tiraścāṃ sambhavaḥ svarge yathā na narake tathā|na pretānāṃ yatastajjaṃ duḥkhaṃ nānubhavanti
te

yadi tatkarmabhistatra bhūtānāṃ sambhavastathā|iṣyate pariṇāmaśca kiṃ vijñānasya neṣyate

karmaṇo vāsanā'nyatra phalamanyatra kalpyate|tatraiva neṣyate yatra vāsanā kiṃ nu kāraṇam

rūpādyāyatanāstitvaṃ tadvineyajanaṃ prati|abhiprāyavaśāduktamupapādukasattvavat

yataḥ svabījād vijñaptiryadābhāsā pravartate|dvividhāyatanatvena te tasyā munirabravīt

tathā pudgalanairātmyapraveśo hi hyanyathā punaḥ|deśanā dharmanairātmyapraveśaḥ kalpitātmanā

na tadekaṃ na cānekaṃ viṣayaḥ paramāṇuśaḥ|na ca te saṃhatā yasmāt paramāṇurna sidhyati

ṣaṭkena yugapadyogāt paramāṇoḥ ṣaḍaṃśatā|ṣaṇṇāṃ samānadeśatvāt piṇḍaḥ syādaṇumātrakaḥ

paramāṇorasaṃyoge tatsaṅghāte'sti kasya saḥ|na cānavayavatvena tatsaṃyogo na sidhyati

digbhāgabhedo yasyāsti tasyaikatvaṃ na yujyate|chāyāvṛtī kathaṃ vā'nyo na piṇḍaścenna tasya
te

ekatve na krameṇetiryugapanna grahāgrahau|vicchinnānekavṛttiśca sūkṣmānīkṣā ca no bhavet

pratyakṣabuddhiḥ svapnādau yathā sā ca yadā tadā|na so'rtho dṛśyate tasya pratyakṣatvaṃ kathaṃ
matam

uktaṃ yathā tadābhāsā vijñaptiḥ smaraṇaṃ tataḥ|svapne dṛgviṣayābhāvaṃ nāprabuddho'vagacchati

anyonyādhipatitvena vijñaptiniyamo mithaḥ|middhenopahataṃ cittaṃ svapne tenāsamaṃ phalam

maraṇaṃ paravijñaptiviśeṣād vikriyā yathā|smṛtilopādikā'nyeṣāṃ piśācādimanovaśāt

kathaṃ vā daṇḍakāraṇyaśūnyatvamṛṣikopataḥ|manodaṇḍo mahāvadyaḥ kathaṃ vā tena sidhyati

paracittavidāṃ jñānamayathārthaṃ kathaṃ yathā|svacittajñānamajñānād yathā buddhasya gocaraḥ

vijñaptimātratāsiddhiḥ svaśaktisadṛśī mayā|kṛteyaṃ sarvathā sā tu na cintyā buddhagocaraḥ

|| viṃśatikākārikāḥ samāptāḥ ||
